What this site contains

This site brings together public geographic data, clinic locations, and research briefs to help visualize where rural health resources sit relative to agricultural and farmworker communities. It is a working prototype—not a clinical directory or an official state resource.

The interactive map shows Washington rural health clinic locations from public state data. Supporting research documents explore regional clusters, public institutional hubs, and ethical approaches to community outreach strategy.

Data & ethics

Clinic locations come from the Washington State Geospatial Open Data Portal / DOH Rural Health Clinics layer. Research materials use public sources only and do not identify private messaging groups, individuals, or sensitive community details.
